Element Collector

How to Play!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four elements below. By clicking each elements you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You defeat the enchantress by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if you go over.

The value of each element is hidden from you until you click it.

The enchantress will reset the number after every battle!
